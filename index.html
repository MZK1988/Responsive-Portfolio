About Me



My name is Max Korenvaes and I have been wanting to learn how to code for a long time. I would like to learn APIs, how the front end of a website connects to the data warehouse, and how to make forms work for my business. I am excited to be surrounded by students that are passionate about learning how to code. What's more, everyone here has the conviction to invest in their future. Cheers to the quest for knowledge and the ever enduring human spirit...until the machines takeover.

I am a principal of a venture capital and private equity investment firm as well as a founding partner of a pathology practice and clinical laboratory management and development company. I sit on the boards of Xhale Assurance, Inc., POP Medical, Inc., and Double Helix Management. I focus on technology, biotechnology, medical devices, healthcare services, diagnostics, and agribiotech. If any of you all have interesting ideas of how a software product can increase work flow efficiencies, I am all ears. My wife's name is Lauren and we have a dog named Stella.<3
